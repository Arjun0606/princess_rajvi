Painterly with gentle gradients and soft shadows. Hand-drawn line work, NOT photo-real, NOT pixel-art, NOT 3D-render. No text, no watermarks, no signatures.

```
CHARACTER REFERENCE — PRINCESS RAJVI:
A young South Asian woman in her mid-twenties. Long flowing dark brown hair. Big warm brown eyes. A small delicate gold tiara with a single rose-cut pink gem at the center. A soft dusty-pink puffy-sleeved princess dress with white lace collar trim that falls to her ankles. A single bright sunflower tucked behind her right ear. Slightly chibi-leaning but not full chibi — gentle stylized proportions. Cheerful, slightly mischievous expression by default.

WORLD REFERENCE — THE TERRACE:
A small private terrace of a pink stone castle. Pale rose marble floor with subtle pink veining. An ornate carved stone balustrade overlooks an endless sunflower meadow stretching to the horizon. A small marble side table on the right edge. A planter of sunflowers on the left edge. Pink rose vines climbing the railing. The castle's pink turrets are visible behind the viewpoint.

I will now ask for each image one by one. Use these references consistently. Confirm you understand.
```
